Electricity For All

\\\///

In a country in which Load-Shedding is raging, there are some solutions
we can implement that will answer to South Africa's social and environmental issues.

In 2023

We plan to fincance and build enough wind turbines to give load-shedding free electricity to over 1000 habitants of the townships of Cape Town. As it was introduced in 2018, they were supposed to be just a temporary messure. As time went, we realise they were here to stay. The habitants of the townships are the ones suffering the most from Load-Shedding since they don't the ressources to buy external batteries or generators like most of the well off citizens of Cape Town bought.

Welcome

The Windmill Project is a Non-Profit Organisation created in 2022 by a three members team. We are trying to solve the electricity problems in South Africa or at least improving the situation.

About Us


\\\///

The Windmill Project. A dream, a vision, an improvement for many. It is possible to make the world a better place, might it be at a smaller scale. This is the essence of the project, the wind turbine technology allows for much flexibility as smaller turbines can be implemented faster and still provide energy for a few hundred people adding some power units to the grid will not only better the lives of few but also improve the resilience of the South African power grid.
As the technology pop-ups in cape town we hope to ease the entry of renewable energy by other local investors and administrations (as many ecological activists explain) that will realise the potential it brings.

Implementation

We expect to be able to install a multi-house turbine (for approximately 20 households) providing them with electricity all year long.

Solidarity

The first wind turbines will be installed in townships; where the population is most affected by the Load Sheddings.

Flexible

We can adapt to the needs of the population with smaller or bigger turbines in order reach villages of different sizes.

Make a Change

The objective of this non-profit is not to control a few wind turbines but to familiarize with this technology and in end solve on of South Africa Great Challenge.

Values

Empowerment

\\\///

We do not only plan to deliver the turbines and potentially let them degrade over the decades. We plan to form some habitants of the township so that they understand the turbines know how to fufill routine maintenance and small repairs. We want the turbines not only to be used by the habitants but also to empower them with the knowledge and pride that they are handling a country wide issues on their own and for their neighbors and relatives.

Quality over Quantity

\\\///

The population of the Townships have to be taken in consideration, not only the authorities should be asked for the permission to cnstruct. Our project has to reflect the population needs as weel as implicating them thoughout the whole process. This will allow them to reproduce the project in the future as they see the advantages. This is why the first Turbine will be of smaller size (15m) in order use as a test-phase. Once done, we should write a protocol so that such innitiatives could pop-up more easily thoughout the coutry.
