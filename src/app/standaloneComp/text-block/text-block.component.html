We do not only plan to deliver the turbines and potentially let them degrade over the decades. We plan to form some habitants of the township so that they understand the turbines know how to fufill routine maintenance and small repairs. We want the turbines not only to be used by the habitants but also to empower them with the knowledge and pride that they are handling a country wide issues on their own and for their neighbors and relatives.
