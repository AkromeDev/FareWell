Am wirksamsten ist die Behandlung als Kur: Weil sich das Gewebe schrittweise verändert, sind mehrere Sitzungen nötig, oft 4–8. The treatment is most effective as a course: because the tissue changes gradually, several sessions are needed, often 4–8. Leave about 2–4 days between appointments so your lymphatic system and metabolism can carry away what has been released.
